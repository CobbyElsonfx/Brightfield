Mentor

We've got an active community of mentors, eager students, and long-time graduates helping each other succeed and be passionate about technology. Mentors truly are the key ingredient to making Brightfied Tech Academy work so well.

"Tell me and I forget, teach me and I may remember, involve me and I learn.” ― Benjamin Franklin

What does it take to be a mentor ?

Online meetings

Attend and be involved with a weekly Zoom meetup (6-8 p.m.) one night per week for 12 weeks..

Provide guidance

Answer questions, lead discussions, review projects, and offer advice to students in a programming language of your choice. Virtually mentor on Slack.

Work together

Work with other mentors to help your class stay on track, involved, and interested.

Be professional

Maintain professionalism and adhere to the Code of Conduct.

“Mentors get to help people learn and have those ‘a-ha’ moments. It’s a really great, enriching experience.”

We are constantly trying new classes, so let us know if you are interested in something special. We regularly offer classes in Front-end Web Development, C#, Python, and JavaScript. If you don't see your preferred stack, reach out and we may be able to create a new class!

“As a mentor you get to work with up-and-coming software developers first hand. It’s a great way to find talented candidates.”

--Senior Mentor : Mr. Oliver Mensah
